GoldenVilla (VILLA) Whitepaper

1. Introduction

GoldenVilla (VILLA) is a meme coin project on the Solana blockchain, but with a premium twist. It merges the joy of meme culture with a luxurious, tropical lifestyle—like a digital vacation in a dream villa.

2. Mission & Vision

Mission: To build a digital ecosystem that values design, creativity, and exclusivity within the meme coin culture.

Vision: To become the most iconic and stylish meme coin on the Solana network, backed by a strong community and technology that's ready for the future—including threats from quantum computing. Long-term, GoldenVilla envisions a world where 1 VILLA token can hold massive value, even being exchanged for a real-world villa.

3. Technology

  • Blockchain: Solana
  • Token Type: SPL Token
  • Fixed Total Supply: 50,000,000 VILLA (no inflation)
  • Smart Contract:
    • Used for token distribution, community tools, and future integrations
    • Built with upgradeable contract architecture to adapt over time
  • Quantum-Ready Vision:
    • Contract framework allows migration to quantum-resistant cryptography when necessary

4. Token Distribution

  • Liquidity Pool: 40% - 20,000,000 VILLA
  • Team & Development: 5% - 2,500,000 VILLA
  • Community & Marketing: 40% - 20,000,000 VILLA

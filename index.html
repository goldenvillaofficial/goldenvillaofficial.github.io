About

GoldenVilla (VILLA) is an elegant meme coin project built on the Solana blockchain. Unlike ordinary meme coins, VILLA combines fun with a premium lifestyle image—bringing the tropical, luxurious, and relaxing atmosphere of a villa holiday to the crypto space.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To create a digital space for a community that values fun, design, and exclusivity within the meme coin ecosystem.

Vision: To become the strongest-branded meme coin with the most loyal community on the Solana network, creating value through creativity and lifestyle.

Technology

  • Blockchain: Solana
  • Token Type: SPL Token
  • Max Supply: 50,000,000 VILLA
  • Smart Contract: No staking or heavy utility features. Focused on community distribution and branding.

Token Distribution

  • Liquidity Pool: 40% – 20,000,000
  • Airdrop & Giveaway: 25% – 12,500,000
  • Team & Development: 15% – 7,500,000
  • Community & Marketing: 10% – 5,000,000
  • Reserve: 10% – 5,000,000

Roadmap

Phase 1 – Foundation:

  • Launch of VILLA token on Solana
  • Design of tropical gold logo and branding
  • One-page website (goldenvilla.io)

Phase 2 – Community:

  • Initial airdrop to early supporters
  • Social media activation
  • Partnerships with content creators and NFT designers

Phase 3 – Expansion:

  • Listing on DEXs such as Jupiter or Raydium
  • Release of VILLA NFT edition
  • Exclusive merchandise (t-shirts, hats, etc.)

Phase 4 – Evolution:

  • Potential integration with metaverse or tropical-themed games
  • Collaborations with other crypto/lifestyle projects

Why Choose VILLA?

  • Unique branding: a blend of meme culture and elegant lifestyle
  • Fast and low-cost: built on Solana
  • Limited supply: only 50 million tokens
  • Community-focused: it's about more than just hype—it's style

Core Team

GoldenVilla is created by an independent creative team with experience in design, crypto, and online communities. The team chooses to remain anonymous to preserve the spirit of memes and decentralization while maintaining professionalism.
